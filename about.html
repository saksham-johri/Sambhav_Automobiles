WHO WE ARE

SAMBHAV AUTOMOBILES has been providing finest auto repair, maintenance and towing services since April 2007 to our wonderful customers in Ghaziabad,UP and the surrounding local communities of DELHI NCR as Maruti Authorized Service Station, and are committed to provide friendly, high quality automotive services to the proud owners of Maruti Vehicles! We take pride in hiring the most Qualified, Certified Maruti trained technicians and utilizing the best automotive technology, Genuine spare parts and diagnostic equipment to service, fix and maintain your vehicle. Whether your vehicle is old or new, we have the finest mechanics standing by to fix any problem you may have. From paid services and warranty-replacements, to tire sales and towing services, and Insurance renewals and cashless settlements with all Maruti tied-up as well as major non tied-up Insurance Companies, our friendly staff can handle it all!
